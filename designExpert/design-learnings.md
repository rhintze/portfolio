# Design Inspiration Log
> A personal record of observations, decisions, and open questions.
> This is NOT a design system. It's a thinking journal.
> Update it when something clicks — not on every session.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS FILE

- **After analyzing a reference app**: Log the 2–3 sharpest observations. Not everything — just what surprised you or explained something you couldn't articulate before.
- **When making a design decision**: Write down what you decided and why. Future you will thank present you.
- **When you consciously reject a pattern**: Log it in "Rejected Patterns" with your reasoning. This is just as valuable as what you adopt.
- **Open questions**: Keep a running list of things you're still figuring out. Revisit them.

This file feeds your thinking, not your codebase.
